ABOUT

  • ICE CREAM, it is the very first name that comes to the mind, when we talk about something sweet.

  • Ice cream is a creamy delight that is made with the perfect mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and other flavouring ingredients.

  • This softly frozen delight has been people’s favourite for many years to now.

  • It is not only popular amongst the kids but the different kinds of ice cream are also popular amongst all age group of users.

  • Mostly, ice cream is purchased by the consumer on basis of flavour and ingredients.

  • There are many different flavours of ice cream manufactured, and to some extent limited only by imagination.

  • Vanilla accounts for 30% of the ice cream consumed.

  • This is partly because it is used in so many products, like milkshakes,
    sundaes, banana splits, in addition to being consumed with pies,
    desserts, etc.
